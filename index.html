This is an index of the Clane Parish baptismal and marriage registers which date from around 1785 to 1921. Much of the original material is legible but sadly due to age and use some of it is not. There are many errors and omissions in these pages some of which I am aware and I don't know how many I am not.
There are some records which appear to be a duplication, I have left these in case, as they sometimes do, refer to two different people.
I've tried as best I can to group the records into families by using a father's surname and that of his spouse, where recorded, to assemble what I hope are the correct families. I have relied on using the spouse's name and to some extent the recorded residence to verify family units. It should be noted that family names were often spelt phonetically so that one person may spell, for instance, Connolly as Connelly, or Conoly, or even Conly.
